Optimized features

Benefit from the flexibility of the Cloud: Zero commitment!

Selfdeploy is THE solution for fast deployment of test, development and production environments for your applications in the Cloud: your security settings are integrated, it’s fast and flexible and you keep control of your costs.

Choice

A wide selection of blueprints and instance types (CPU/RAM)

Security

A predefined backup policy based on daily snapshots; you can generate and manage a manual backup at any time. Different possible actions are possible on theses snapshots such as restore, disaster recovery and clone.

Performance

Monitoring is available for all machines, you can analyze the behavior of your resources through graphs of CPU, RAM, disk space, IO, availability..

Control

You define in advance the scheduling of your virtual machines.
